Santorini

Santorini is a breathtaking Greek island famous for its whitewashed buildings, blue-domed churches, stunning sunsets, and unique volcanic beaches.

Athens

Athens, historic city and capital of Greece. Many of Classical civilization's intellectual and artistic ideas originated there, and the city is generally considered to be the birthplace of Western civilization.

Crete

The largest island in Greece, Crete is a diverse and vibrant land packed with ancient ruins, buzzing cities and breathtaking beaches.
